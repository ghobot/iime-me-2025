Virtual Anatomy

Navigating Virtual Cadavers

Students will navigate scanned plastinated models on any device including in VR via Sketchfab. Understanding the 3D spatial relationships between structures, a traditional challenge of navigating cadavers, is improved in VR and AR.

Animated Anatomy

One advantage of virtual simulations of anatomy over traditional cadavers is the opportunity to understand how structure informs function and physiology and disease. In this highly detailed simulation of the heart, students will see the organ's normal processes and the pathology of several heart diseases.

Accessibilty to rare learning spaces.

We are introducing exclusive learning spaces like the pathology lab to show students how anatomy and pathology are intrinsic to diagnosis. In this immersive video able to be viewed in VR, a pathologist examines real tissue and describes the likelihood of polyps being cancerous.
