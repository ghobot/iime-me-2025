A Project I am working on: Augmented Anatomy

Augmented Reality Cadavers


Mobility is a big part of our education technology strategy. With Sketchfab and the digital Body atlas Complete Anatomy students can use Augmented Reality on mobile devices to visualize complex anatomy.

Navigating Virtual Cadavers


Students will navigate scanned plastinated models on any device including in VR via Sketchfab and digital cadavers using Organon VR. Understanding the 3D spatial relationships between structures, a traditional challenge of navigating cadavers, is improved using VR and AR.

Animated Anatomy


One difference of virtual simulations of anatomy over traditional cadavers is the opportunity to understand how structure informs function and physiology and disease. In this highly detailed simulation of the heart from Sharecare VR, students will see the organ's normal processes and the pathology of several heart diseases.
